How Crop Health Changed Through the Season
Monthly NDVI satellite health scores · Nov 2022 – Apr 2023 · healthy vs stressed district
What is NDVI?NDVI (Normalised Difference Vegetation Index) is a 0–1 score from satellite infrared light measuring how green and photosynthetically active crops are. Above 0.7 = lush, healthy wheat. Below 0.5 in February = serious trouble. It's a satellite "health thermometer" for crops. Source: ESA Sentinel-2 SR, Band 8 (NIR) and Band 4 (Red).
